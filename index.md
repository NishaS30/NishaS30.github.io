I am a [PhD student](https://www.math.tifrbng.res.in/people/nisha2020) at [Tata Institute of Fundamental Research - Centre for Applicable Mathematics](https://www.math.tifrbng.res.in/). I am working in the field of Inverse problems under the guidance of [Prof. Venky Krishnan](https://venkykrishnan.tifrbng.res.in/home).

I have analyzed range characterization and unique continuation property for several transforms such as _spherical mean transform_ and _$$d$$-plane transform_ in the Euclidean setting. I am interested in the analysis of these transforms and other transforms such as _geodesic ray transform_ in the Riemannian manifold setting.
